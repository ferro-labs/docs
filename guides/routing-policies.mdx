---
title: Routing policies
description: Choose how requests are routed across providers.
---

The gateway supports multiple routing strategies to balance cost, latency, and reliability.

## Single

Route all requests to one provider or model.

## Fallback

Retry a secondary provider if the primary fails, with exponential backoff.

## Weighted

Split traffic across providers by percentage.

## Conditional

Route based on request metadata or model selection.

Use routing policies to control cost and reduce downtime during provider outages.
